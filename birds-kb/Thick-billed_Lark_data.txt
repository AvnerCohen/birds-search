<p>The <b>thick-billed lark</b> (<i>Ramphocoris clotbey</i>) or <b>Clotbey lark</b> is a species of <a href="/wiki/Lark" title="Lark">lark</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Alaudidae" class="mw-redirect" title="Alaudidae">Alaudidae</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Taxonomy_and_systematics">Taxonomy and systematics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Thick-billed_lark&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Taxonomy and systematics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was named after <a href="/wiki/Antoine_Clot" title="Antoine Clot">Antoine Clot</a>. It was originally described as belonging to the genus <i><a href="/wiki/Melanocorypha" title="Melanocorypha">Melanocorypha</a></i> and is now placed in the <a href="/wiki/Monotypic" class="mw-redirect" title="Monotypic">monotypic</a> genus <i><b>Ramphocoris</b></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution_and_habitat">Distribution and habitat</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Thick-billed_lark&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Distribution and habitat">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is found in northern Africa from Mauritania and Morocco to Libya, also in central regions of the <a href="/wiki/Arabian_Peninsula" title="Arabian Peninsula">Arabian Peninsula</a>. Its natural <a href="/wiki/Habitat" title="Habitat">habitats</a> are <a href="/wiki/Subtropical" class="mw-redirect" title="Subtropical">subtropical</a> or <a href="/wiki/Tropical" class="mw-redirect" title="Tropical">tropical</a> dry <a href="/wiki/Shrubland" title="Shrubland">shrubland</a> and hot <a href="/wiki/Desert" title="Desert">deserts</a>.
</p>
