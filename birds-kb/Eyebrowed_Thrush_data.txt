<p>The <b>eyebrowed thrush</b> (<i>Turdus obscurus</i>) is a member of the <a href="/wiki/Thrush_(bird)" title="Thrush (bird)">thrush</a> family Turdidae. The scientific name comes from <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a> <i>Turdus</i>, "thrush" and <i>obscurus</i> "dark".<sup id="cite_ref-job_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-job-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG/220px-Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG" decoding="async" width="220" height="342" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG/330px-Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG/440px-Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG 2x" data-file-width="2227" data-file-height="3459" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Turdus_obscurus_MWNH_1761.JPG" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Egg, Collection <a href="/wiki/Museum_Wiesbaden" title="Museum Wiesbaden">Museum Wiesbaden</a></div></div></div>
<p>It breeds in dense <a href="/wiki/Conifer" class="mw-redirect" title="Conifer">coniferous</a> forest and <a href="/wiki/Taiga" title="Taiga">taiga</a> eastwards from <a href="/wiki/Siberia" title="Siberia">Siberia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mongolia" title="Mongolia">Mongolia</a> to <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. It is strongly <a href="/wiki/Bird_migration" title="Bird migration">migratory</a>, wintering south to China and <a href="/wiki/Southeast_Asia" title="Southeast Asia">Southeast Asia</a>. It is a rare vagrant to western <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>.
</p><p>It nests in trees, laying 4-6 <a href="/wiki/Egg_(biology)" class="mw-redirect" title="Egg (biology)">eggs</a> in a neat nest. Migrating birds and wintering birds often form small flocks. It is omnivorous, eating a wide range of <a href="/wiki/Insect" title="Insect">insects</a>, <a href="/wiki/Earthworm" title="Earthworm">earthworms</a> and <a href="/wiki/Berry" title="Berry">berries</a>.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg/220px-Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg/330px-Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg/440px-Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2196" data-file-height="1464" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Eyebrowed_Thrush_from_Khonoma_bird_watching_tour_to_Nagaland.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>From <a href="/wiki/Khonoma" title="Khonoma">Khonoma</a> village in <a href="/wiki/Nagaland" title="Nagaland">Nagaland</a>, <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>This is an attractive thrush, with a grey back and head, the latter having a black eyeline, bordered white above and below. The breast and flanks are orange, and the belly white. The sexes are fairly similar, but immatures have a browner back.
</p><p>The male has a simple whistling song, similar to the related <a href="/wiki/Mistle_thrush" title="Mistle thrush">mistle thrush</a>.
</p><p>In 2007 an eyebrowed thrush was sighted at the <a href="/wiki/Jerusalem_Bird_Observatory" title="Jerusalem Bird Observatory">Jerusalem Bird Observatory</a> in <a href="/wiki/Jerusalem" title="Jerusalem">Jerusalem</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This is the second recorded sighting in <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>; the first was at <a href="/wiki/Eilat" title="Eilat">Eilat</a> in October 1996.
</p><p>In 2011, an eyebrowed thrush was sighted in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Malanda" class="mw-redirect" title="Malanda">Malanda</a> in <a href="/wiki/Queensland" title="Queensland">Queensland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> This is possibly the first confirmed sighting of the species on the Australian mainland.
</p>
