<p>The <b>white-cheeked tern</b> (<i>Sterna repressa</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Tern" title="Tern">tern</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Laridae" title="Laridae">Laridae</a>.
It is found around the coasts on the <a href="/wiki/Red_Sea" title="Red Sea">Red Sea</a>, around the <a href="/wiki/Horn_of_Africa" title="Horn of Africa">Horn of Africa</a> to <a href="/wiki/Kenya" title="Kenya">Kenya</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Persian_Gulf" title="Persian Gulf">Persian Gulf</a> and along the <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iranian</a> coast to <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistan</a> and western <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ioc_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ioc-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behaviour">Behaviour</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=White-cheeked_tern&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behaviour">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Most of this species is migratory, although those in <a href="/wiki/East_Africa" title="East Africa">East Africa</a> may remain there all year. It breeds in colonies of 10–200 pairs. These colonies can consist of a mixture of tern species.<sup id="cite_ref-IUCN_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IUCN-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Habitat">Habitat</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=White-cheeked_tern&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Habitat">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The species inhabits tropical coasts and inshore waters, foraging mainly within 3 kilometres (1.9&#160;mi) of land over <a href="/wiki/Coral_reefs" class="mw-redirect" title="Coral reefs">coral reefs</a>. Its nest is a shallow scrape on rock, sand, gravel or coral islands, bare and exposed sandflats and sparsely vegetated open ground on sand-dunes and above the high-water mark on beaches.<sup id="cite_ref-IUCN_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IUCN-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Diet">Diet</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=White-cheeked_tern&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Diet">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Its diet consists of <a href="/wiki/Invertebrates" class="mw-redirect" title="Invertebrates">invertebrates</a> and small <a href="/wiki/Fish" title="Fish">fish</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-IUCN_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IUCN-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
