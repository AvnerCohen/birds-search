<p><i><b>Winter VIsitor</b></i> (Visitante de Invierno) (<a href="/wiki/2008_in_film" title="2008 in film">2008</a>) is an <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentine</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spanish</a> film, written and directed by <a href="/wiki/Sergio_Esquenazi" title="Sergio Esquenazi">Sergio Esquenazi</a>.  It is probably the most important Argentine horror film of the last 30 years, since it was the first Argentine horror film in  two decades to be supported by INCAA (National Institute of Cinema and Audiovisual Arts) and therefore the first Argentine horror film to premiere in the country in 20 years, which opened the doors to directors and producers of the genre which up to that time were condemned to an under circuit.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Visitor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A 20 years old Ariel Lambert, after a suicide attempt, is sent with her mother to a small summer town in the state of Buenos Aires. Ariel arrives in winter, a time of the year in which the village is almost deserted. From his room and with the help of a telescope, Ariel witness a man taking children into his house, children that will not ever come out again. Ariel tries to call the police, but given his precarious mental state, they will not take him seriously. Determined to prove what he saw is true, he set forth in search for the evidence required to prove that what he saw is not only part of his imagination but a horrible truth.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Visitor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Director <a href="/wiki/Sergio_Esquenazi" title="Sergio Esquenazi">Sergio Esquenazi</a> has said in media interviews that the story is based on his travels in winter to Pinamar. Forest surrounded by fog and a town with deserted streets and houses were the source his inspiration.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Filming_location">Filming location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Visitor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Filming location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The film was shot entirely in Pinamar, <a href="/wiki/Buenos_Aires_Province" title="Buenos Aires Province">Buenos Aires Province</a>, Argentina.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution">Distribution</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Visitor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Distribution">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The film was first featured at the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Fantasporto_International_Film_Festival&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Fantasporto International Film Festival (page does not exist)">Fantasporto International Film Festival</a>. It opened in Argentina on March, 2008.
</p><p>The film was also shown at various film festivals, including: the <a href="/wiki/Toronto_International_Film_Festival" title="Toronto International Film Festival">Toronto International Film Festival</a>; the <a href="/wiki/New_York_Film_Festival" title="New York Film Festival">New York Film Festival</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Havana_Film_Festival" title="Havana Film Festival">Havana Film Festival</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">Cuba</a>.
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>, it was presented at the <a href="/wiki/New_York_Film_Festival" title="New York Film Festival">New York Film Festival</a> on October 2, 2008.  It also opened in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a> on October 12, 2008.
</p>
