<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/Vinous" title="Vinous" class="mw-redirect">Vinous</a>-breasted Starling</b> (<i><b>Sturnus burmannicus</b></i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Starling" title="Starling">starling</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Sturnidae" title="Sturnidae" class="mw-redirect">Sturnidae</a> family. It is sometimes placed in the <a href="/wiki/Myna" title="Myna">myna</a> genus <i><a href="/wiki/Acridotheres" title="Acridotheres">Acridotheres</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="editsection">[<a href="/w/index.php?title=Vinous-breasted_Starling&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Distribution and habitat">edit</a>]</span> <span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution_and_habitat">Distribution and habitat</span></h2>
<p>It is found in <a href="/wiki/Cambodia" title="Cambodia">Cambodia</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, <a href="/wiki/Laos" title="Laos">Laos</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Burma" title="Burma">Burma</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thailand" title="Thailand">Thailand</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Vietnam" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>. In the early 2000s it has become an <a href="/wiki/Invasive_species" title="Invasive species">invasive species</a> in <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>, alongside the <a href="/wiki/Common_Myna" title="Common Myna">Common Myna</a> that invaded a few years earlier. Both populations seem to have started from individuals that escaped a bird park in Tel-Aviv.</p>
