<p><i><b>Alaemon</b></i> is a genus of <a href="/wiki/Lark" title="Lark">larks</a>, also known as the <b>hoopoe-larks</b> (though this name, without the <a href="/wiki/Hyphen" title="Hyphen">hyphen</a>, also refers to the <a href="/wiki/Greater_Hoopoe-Lark" title="Greater Hoopoe-Lark">Greater Hoopoe-Lark</a>). It has two species:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Greater_Hoopoe-Lark" title="Greater Hoopoe-Lark">Greater Hoopoe-Lark</a>, <i>Alaemon alaudipes</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lesser_Hoopoe-Lark" title="Lesser Hoopoe-Lark">Lesser Hoopoe-Lark</a>, <i>Alaemon hamertoni</i></li>
</ul>
