<p>The <b>Spectacled Bulbul</b> (<i>Pycnonotus erythropthalmos</i>), also known as the <b>Lesser Brown Bulbul</b>, is a bird in the <a href="/wiki/Bulbul" title="Bulbul">bulbul</a> <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a>. It is known in <a href="/wiki/Malay_Language" title="Malay Language" class="mw-redirect">Malay</a> as <i>Merbah Kecil</i>. It is endemic to <a href="/wiki/Southeast_Asia" title="Southeast Asia">Southeast Asia</a>.</p>
