<p>The <b>Persian shearwater</b> (<i>Puffinus persicus</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Seabird" title="Seabird">seabird</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Procellariidae" title="Procellariidae">Procellariidae</a> formerly lumped in with <a href="/wiki/Audubon%27s_shearwater" title="Audubon&#39;s shearwater">Audubon's shearwater</a> (<i>Puffinus lherminieri</i>).<sup id="cite_ref-IOC_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IOC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Subspecies">Subspecies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Persian_shearwater&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Subspecies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are two listed <a href="/wiki/Subspecies" title="Subspecies">subspecies</a> of the Persian shearwater:<sup id="cite_ref-IOC_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IOC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li><i>P. p. persicus</i> – <small>(<a href="/wiki/Allan_Octavian_Hume" title="Allan Octavian Hume">Hume</a>, 1872)</small>: breeds in the <a href="/wiki/Khuriya_Muriya_Islands" title="Khuriya Muriya Islands">Khuriya Muriya Islands</a>, (<a href="/wiki/Oman" title="Oman">Oman</a>) &amp; <a href="/wiki/Socotra" title="Socotra">Socotra</a>.</li>
<li><i>P. p. temptator</i> – <small>(<a href="/wiki/Michel_Louette" title="Michel Louette">Louette</a> &amp; Herremans, 1985)</small>: breeds in the <a href="/wiki/Comoros" title="Comoros">Comoros</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Range">Range</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Persian_shearwater&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Range">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After breeding, the northern subspecies ranges from the southern <a href="/wiki/Red_Sea" title="Red Sea">Red Sea</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Aden" title="Gulf of Aden">Gulf of Aden</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Somalia" title="Somalia">Somali</a> coast across the south of the <a href="/wiki/Arabian_Peninsula" title="Arabian Peninsula">Arabian Peninsula</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Oman" title="Gulf of Oman">Gulf of Oman</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistan</a> and western <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Redman_Stevenson_Fanshawe_2016_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Redman_Stevenson_Fanshawe_2016-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The southern subspecies stays in the area around the <a href="/wiki/Comoros" title="Comoros">Comoros</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Tanzania" title="Tanzania">Tanzanian</a> and northern <a href="/wiki/Mozambique" title="Mozambique">Mozambican</a> coast.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Population">Population</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Persian_shearwater&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Population">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The nominate subspecies is thought to number in the thousands of pairs on the Khuriya Muriya Islands off Oman and Socotra, Yemen, while <i>P. p. temptator</i> has been estimated to number fewer than 500 pairs on <a href="/wiki/Moheli" class="mw-redirect" title="Moheli">Moheli</a> Island in the Comoros (reviewed by Brooke 2004). It is thought likely that more breeding colonies lie undiscovered elsewhere in the northern <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">Indian Ocean</a> (Onley and Scofield 2007), thus the total population probably includes more than 10,000 mature individuals. The population trend is believed to be downwards.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
