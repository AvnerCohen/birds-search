<p>The <b>Soft-plumaged Petrel</b> (<i>Pterodroma mollis</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Seabird" title="Seabird">seabird</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Procellariidae" title="Procellariidae">Procellariidae</a> family.</p>

<h2><span class="editsection">[<a href="/w/index.php?title=Soft-plumaged_Petrel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Distribution">edit</a>]</span> <span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution">Distribution</span></h2>
<p>It breeds on islands in the <a href="/wiki/Southern_Hemisphere" title="Southern Hemisphere">Southern Hemisphere</a>, nesting on <a href="/wiki/Tristan_da_Cunha" title="Tristan da Cunha">Tristan da Cunha</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gough_Island" title="Gough Island">Gough Island</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Prince_Edward_Islands" title="Prince Edward Islands">Prince Edward Islands</a>, <a href="/wiki/Crozet_Islands" title="Crozet Islands">Crozet Islands</a>, <a href="/wiki/Macquarie_Island" title="Macquarie Island">Macquarie Island</a>, and on the <a href="/wiki/Antipodes_Islands" title="Antipodes Islands">Antipodes Islands</a> of <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a>. Small numers breed in the <a href="/wiki/Maatsuyker_Islands" title="Maatsuyker Islands">Maatsuyker Island Group</a> of southern <a href="/wiki/Tasmania" title="Tasmania">Tasmania</a>. It disperses outside the breeding season, reaching eastern <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a> north to <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>, southern <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>. It has occurred as a <a href="/wiki/Vagrancy_in_birds" title="Vagrancy in birds" class="mw-redirect">vagrant</a> in <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jordan" title="Jordan">Jordan</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="editsection">[<a href="/w/index.php?title=Soft-plumaged_Petrel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Taxonomy">edit</a>]</span> <span class="mw-headline" id="Taxonomy">Taxonomy</span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Fea%27s_Petrel" title="Fea's Petrel">Fea's Petrel</a> (<i>P. feae</i>), <a href="/wiki/Deserta%27s_Petrel" title="Deserta's Petrel" class="mw-redirect">Deserta's Petrel</a> (<i>P. desertae</i>) and <a href="/wiki/Zino%27s_Petrel" title="Zino's Petrel">Zino's Petrel</a> (<i>P. madeira</i>) of the North Atlantic were formerly treated as <a href="/wiki/Subspecies" title="Subspecies">subspecies</a> of this bird.</p>
