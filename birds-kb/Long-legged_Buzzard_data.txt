<p>The <b>long-legged buzzard</b> (<i>Buteo rufinus</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Bird_of_prey" title="Bird of prey">bird of prey</a> in the genus <i>Buteo</i>. It is similar in appearance to the <a href="/wiki/Rough-legged_buzzard" title="Rough-legged buzzard">rough-legged buzzard</a> (<i>Buteo lagopus</i>), but it is larger and more robust.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Long-legged_buzzard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Long-legged_Buzzard_(24521858347).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0a/Long-legged_Buzzard_%2824521858347%29.jpg/220px-Long-legged_Buzzard_%2824521858347%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0a/Long-legged_Buzzard_%2824521858347%29.jpg/330px-Long-legged_Buzzard_%2824521858347%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0a/Long-legged_Buzzard_%2824521858347%29.jpg/440px-Long-legged_Buzzard_%2824521858347%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1124" data-file-height="753" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Long-legged_Buzzard_(24521858347).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>A wintering bird near <a href="/wiki/Jaisalmer" title="Jaisalmer">Jaisalmer</a> in <a href="/wiki/Rajasthan" title="Rajasthan">Rajasthan</a>, India</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg/220px-ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="348" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg/330px-ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg/440px-ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg 2x" data-file-width="909" data-file-height="1438" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:ButeoFeroxKeulemans.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Illustration by <a href="/wiki/John_Gerrard_Keulemans" title="John Gerrard Keulemans">Keulemans</a>, 1874</div></div></div>
<p>This is one of the largest species of <i><a href="/wiki/Buteo" title="Buteo">Buteo</a></i>. Length can range from 50 to 66&#160;cm (20 to 26&#160;in) and wingspan from 115 to 160&#160;cm (45 to 63&#160;in). Females, at an average mass of 1.3&#160;kg (2.9&#160;lb), are larger than males, at an average of 1.1&#160;kg (2.4&#160;lb).<sup id="cite_ref-europeanraptors_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-europeanraptors-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Stevenson2001_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Stevenson2001-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> There are many different colour forms, but usually long-leggeds have a clear orange tint to the plumage, red or orange tail, pale head and largely white underwings. There is usually a distinctive black carpal patch and dark trailing edge to the wing. The rump and "trousers" are often dark or deep rufous. Plumage varies from ghostly pale individuals to very dark ones. Some plumages are almost similar to those of the steppe buzzard, the eastern <a href="/wiki/Subspecies" title="Subspecies">subspecies</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Common_buzzard" title="Common buzzard">common buzzard</a> (<i>Buteo buteo vulpinus</i>), but long-legged buzzards have longer wings and are more like rough-legged buzzards or even a small <a href="/wiki/Aquila_(genus)" title="Aquila (genus)">Aquila</a> <a href="/wiki/Eagle" title="Eagle">eagle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-europeanraptors_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-europeanraptors-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Stevenson2001_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Stevenson2001-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution_and_habitat">Distribution and habitat</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Long-legged_buzzard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Distribution and habitat">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It inhabits dry open plains of northern <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>, southeastern <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, west and central <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a> east to <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, and across central <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>. Recent sightings indicate that there is a small population in the Apulian region of south-eastern Italy. Open, uncultivated areas, with high bushes, trees, cliffs or hillocks are favoured as nesting areas. Younger birds disperse north of breeding grounds and there are records from Northern Europe. The breeding population in Greece is around 60 pairs.<sup id="cite_ref-BWP_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BWP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The African Long-legged Buzzard <i>Buteo rufinus cirtensis</i> has recently colonised Europe due to the climate in southern Europe becoming more suitable for this species.  It is expected that the Iberian Peninsula will provide favourable breeding habitat for the African Long-legged Buzzard and facilitate its northward expansion through Europe.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Reforestation in the Judean Mountains in Israel and the West Bank is displacing populations of the long-legged buzzard, and the species is moving into the Judean Foothills and competing with the <a href="/wiki/Short-toed_snake_eagle" title="Short-toed snake eagle">short-toed snake eagle</a> there.<sup id="cite_ref-sciencedaily_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sciencedaily-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Feeding">Feeding</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Long-legged_buzzard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Feeding">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It feeds mostly on small rodents, although it will also take lizards, snakes, small birds and large insects.<sup id="cite_ref-BWP_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BWP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Subspecies">Subspecies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Long-legged_buzzard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Subspecies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ed/Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg/220px-Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="171" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ed/Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg/330px-Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ed/Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg/440px-Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4252" data-file-height="3307" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Buteo_rufinus_cirtensis_MHNT.ZOO.2010.11.90.3.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><i>Buteo rufinus cirtensis</i> - <a href="/wiki/MHNT" class="mw-redirect" title="MHNT">MHNT</a></div></div></div>
<p>There are two <a href="/wiki/Subspecies" title="Subspecies">subspecies</a> recognised:<sup id="cite_ref-Avibase_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Avibase-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li><i>Buteo rufinus rufinus</i>: <a href="/wiki/The_Balkans" class="mw-redirect" title="The Balkans">The Balkans</a> east to <a href="/wiki/Mongolia" title="Mongolia">Mongolia</a> and <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>; winters in <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>.</li>
<li><i>Buteo rufinus cirtensis</i>: Northern Africa from <a href="/wiki/Mauritania" title="Mauritania">Mauritania</a> east to <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Arabian_Peninsula" title="Arabian Peninsula">Arabian Peninsula</a>.</li></ul>
<p>The North African subspecies has recently been recorded breeding in southern <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BB_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BB-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
